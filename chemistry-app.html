Chemistry Practice App

Year: 2020

Description: A mobile app created for Russian chemistry students, designed to help them practice textbook questions. The app allowed students to generate tests from a large bank of questions, making it easier for them to prepare for exams. Though the app is no longer available on Google Play, it was developed using Android Studio and Java.

Platform: Android

Tech Stack: Android Studio, Java
